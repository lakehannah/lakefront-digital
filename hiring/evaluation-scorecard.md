## Scoring Guidelines

| Score | Recommendation |
|-------|------------------|
| **60-70** | Strong hire — offer immediately |
| **50-59** | Hire — good candidate with minor gaps |
| **40-49** | Consider — has potential but significant concerns |
| **Below 40** | Do not hire — fundamental mismatch |

**Automatic Disqualifiers:**
- Unwilling to do cold outreach
- Uncomfortable with commission structure
- History of not hitting quotas (if experienced)
- Negative references
- Unprofessional behavior in interview

**Automatic Green Flags:**
- Brings leads/referrals to first interview
- Has sold similar products successfully
- Deep network in target market
- Shows up early, prepared
- Asks insightful questions about growth path
