## Usage Tips

**Cold Email Best Practices:**
- Send Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM
- Keep under 150 words
- One clear call-to-action
- Follow up 2-3 times max

**Subject Line Tips:**
- Use their business name
- Ask a question
- Create curiosity

**Tracking:**
- Use Gmail read receipts
- Note response rates
- A/B test subject lines

---

## Customization Guide

**Always customize:**
- [Business Name]
- [Owner Name]
- [Business Type]
- [City]
- [Similar Business] example
- [Amount] pricing ($5K-8K or $10K-15K)
- [Date] references

**Never send generic templates** — customize every email.
