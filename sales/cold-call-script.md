# LAKEFRONT DIGITAL COLD CALL SCRIPT

**Your Goal:** Get a 10-minute discovery call scheduled

---

## THE OPENER (15 seconds)

**"Hi [Name], this is Hannah from Lakefront Digital. I help contractors in Spanish Fork get found by customers who are already looking for them. Quick question — do you have a minute?"**

**If YES:** Continue  
**If NO:** "When's a better time? I'm free [tomorrow morning/afternoon]." (Get specific time)

---

## DISCOVERY QUESTIONS (2-3 minutes)

**"I noticed [Business Name] — do you have a website right now?"**

**If NO website:**
- "How are customers finding you right now?"
- "Are you losing jobs to competitors who show up on Google?"

**If YES, but old/bad:**
- "How's that working for you?"
- "Can customers actually find you when they search [your service] Spanish Fork?"
- "When someone Googles '[service] near me,' do you show up?"

**"What's your biggest challenge getting new customers?"**
- Listen for: referrals drying up, word-of-mouth not enough, competition

**"Who's your ideal customer?"**
- Homeowners? Commercial? Specific neighborhoods?

---

## THE PITCH (1 minute)

**"Here's what we do:"**

**"We build professional websites and apps for small businesses — and we do it fast. Not 3 months like agencies. Weeks."**

**"But here's the difference: We give you an AI interface so you can update your own site. Change photos, update hours, add promotions — without calling a developer or paying $150/hour."**

**"And when you need human help, you get it. 24-48 hour turnaround."**

**"Investment is $5,000-8,000 for a professional site, or $10,000-15,000 if you want the AI management tools. Plus $300-600/month maintenance that includes hosting, updates, and support."**

---

## THE CLOSE (30 seconds)

**"I'd love to show you what this looks like for [Business Name]. Can we schedule a quick 10-minute call this week? I'm flexible — what's better, Tuesday or Thursday?"**

**If they hesitate:**
- "No obligation, just a conversation. If it doesn't make sense, no hard feelings."
- "Worst case, you get some ideas for your current site."

**If they say NO:**
- "Totally understand. Can I check back in a month? Things change fast."
- Get permission to follow up.

---

## OBJECTION HANDLING

**"I already have a website."**
> "Great! Is it bringing you customers? When's the last time you updated it? A lot of businesses have 'set it and forget it' sites that don't actually work. Worth a 10-minute chat to see if it's doing its job?"

**"I can't afford $5,000."**
> "I get it — that's real money. But how many customers are you losing by not being found? If a $5,000 site brings you 2-3 customers worth $3,000 each, it pays for itself. Want to see the math?"

**"I need to think about it."**
> "Absolutely. What specifically are you thinking about? Happy to answer any questions now so you have what you need."

**"Just send me information."**
> "Happy to. Can I email it to [email]? And while I have you — what's the #1 thing you'd want a website to do for you?"

**"I'm not the decision maker."**
> "No problem. Who should I talk to? Can you connect us or give me their info?"

**"We're too busy right now."**
> "That's actually a good problem to have! But busy seasons don't last forever. Want to line this up for [next month] so you're ready when things slow down?"

---

## ENDING THE CALL

**Always:**
- Thank them for their time
- Confirm next steps ("So I'll email you [day] and follow up next week")
- Get their email if you don't have it
- Send them something (even just your website link)

**Sample close:**
> "Thanks for the chat, [Name]. I'll send you our info and a link to see our work. If you have any questions, just reply to that email or text me. Can I check back next Tuesday?"

---

## DAILY GOALS

- **10 calls minimum**
- **2-3 conversations** (not voicemail)
- **1 discovery call scheduled**

**Track in your CRM:**
- Who you called
- What they said
- Next action
- Date to follow up

---

## REMEMBER

- **You're helping, not selling.** They need customers. You help them get found.
- **Be brief.** Respect their time.
- **Ask questions.** Don't pitch blind.
- **Get the meeting.** 10 minutes is all you need.
- **Follow up.** Most sales happen after the 3rd-5th touch.

**You've got this.** 🚀
